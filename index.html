Nano Advenctures of Silver and Gold

About

The Adventures of Nano Silver and Gold is a game to spark childrens interest in Nano-Science. The game starts off with a lesson for the user to read followed by the gameplay where they will implement the information that they have learned. After the first level of the lesson, the player will gain a little more information on the current topic and play another level that implements the new information. This goes on for a few levels and then the player must answer multiple choice questions to prove that they know the information from the lesson. After the questions, the player is free to move on to the next lesson plan.
